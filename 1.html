Action

Make Change for Climate Change

Crabtree & Evelyn

26 Nov 2019 18:30 - 20:30

Crabtree and Evelyn Islington Townhouse 294 Upper Street Islington London N1 2TU

With so much currently in the news about climate change and the realisation that change is needed in this area for us to thrive as a society, it can be confusing to know what needs to change and what we can do to contribute to this. Often it seems that the options available mean we may have to give up things that we love such as holidays or food choices. Or we can feel that it can be too difficult to maintain good intentions we set in the long term. We can end up feeling guilty, disempowered and anxious amid the increasing pressure for change. The good news is, it doesn’t have to be this way! It is possible to feel positive and empowered about what we can do to help. We can actively contribute to the creation of a future for ourselves and our loved ones that respects the natural environment while feeling good about the impact we are making. Sound good? If so, this coaching workshop is for you. It will help you to find what is important to you in the context of climate change, so you can feel more confident and empowered to take action to increase your own wellbeing and that of others. Through a combination of information, practical exercises and discussion you will feel ready to take the next steps to positively contribute towards this important issue. This workshop won’t focus on a deep dive into the science of climate change but it will give an overview to set the context. Its main aim is in helping you to connect with the best ways for you to make changes in this area.
