European Science Café ft. The Climate Reality Project

Goethe-Zentrum Atlanta

20 Nov 2019 18:00 - 20:00

Goethe-Zentrum/German Cultural Center Atlanta 1201 Peachtree Street Northeast Atlanta, GA 30361 United States

The climate crisis affects all of us - and it’s up to all of us to solve it! You are invited to join us for an informative evening at the Goethe-Zentrum Atlanta on November 20, 2019, from 6 pm – 8 pm The German Consulate General partnered with the Goethe-Zentrum Atlanta and the Climate Reality Project for November’s European Science Café Event. We will screen a German, English-subtitled, version of the Truth in 10 slideshow - a 10-minute presentation based on the popular and informative An Inconvenient Truth Presentation by former VP Al Gore. Its simple message: the climate crisis is urgent, but the solutions are at hand. After the screening, the Atlanta Chapter of the Climate Reality will feature a Panel Discussion on Climate Change, moderated by Sarah Backhouse. The topic of the Panel will be Climate Change - its effects and what we can do to face those challenges. The event is free and open to the public The Goethe-Zentrum is located at Colony Square in Midtown Atlanta (1201 Peachtree St NE, Atlanta, GA 30361). We would be honored to have you at this event
