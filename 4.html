Autumn Gathering - Climate Change & the Power of Trees

Women’s Environmental Network

12 Nov 2019 10:30 - 15:00

The Hub Building Victoria Park London E9 7DD

It’s Autumn and things are cooling down. But, around the world temperatures are going up as our climate changes. At our gathering we’ll talk about climate change, and the power that planting, especially tree planting, has to contribute to tackling the problem. We’ll explore some of the beautiful trees of Victoria Park, discuss how you can plant and care for trees near you, and much more! We’ll have our usual free and delicious lunch. Plus, it’s a great chance to meet other people from the wonderful Tower Hamlets Food Growing Network.
